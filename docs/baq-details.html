Migration of the control system for the Beer Elaboration Area

The control systems of the brewing, fermentation, and services areas of the brewery were migrated. S7-400 series of Siemens PLCs were used for the brewing area and S7-1500 series for the cold block. The programming was done using Simatic Step7, Tia Portal 15, and Braumat 7.5.
It was necessary to carry out a start-up in record time to return the factory to its normal operation and achieve the productivity it had prior to the system modification.

I programmed most of the fermentation sequences and led the start-up of this area. I also supported and provided assistance for the start-up of the brewing migration. With this project, I had the challenge of testing multiple processes simultaneously, monitoring them, and fine-tuning all sequences within very short time. I was in constant contact with factory personnel to address the needs of the production process and provided training to the maintenance staff on the new technology."
